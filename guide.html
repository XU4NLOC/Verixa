How to Invest
1
Open Asset Marketplace and browse available assets.
2
Select one asset to open Asset Detail.
3
Read yield, tenor, risk, SME, and buyer information.
4
Click Invest.
5
System opens Invest Flow.
6
Enter investment amount.
7
Review preview: issued tokens, expected payout, and profit.
8
Double-check all details.
9
Click confirm to complete investment.
10
Asset position is added to Portfolio.
11
Hold the asset until maturity.
12
When buyer pays, settlement is executed.
13
Principal and profit are returned to investor wallet.

Key Terms
Credit Rating
AAA - Very low risk, strongest repayment capacity.
AA - Low risk, very strong repayment capacity.
A - Low-to-moderate risk, strong repayment ability.
BBB - Medium risk, adequate repayment capacity.
BB - Elevated risk, speculative profile.
B - High risk, weak repayment resilience.
Note:Credit rating applies to the Buyer (payer) and can be sourced from third-party bureaus or internal scoring systems.
