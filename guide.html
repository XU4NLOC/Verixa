Platform Overview

VIFC tokenizes verified trade receivables so investors can fund short-term invoices and receive fixed returns at maturity. The platform combines KYC, verification, asset review, tokenized investing, secondary exits, and settlement in one client-side workflow.

How to Invest
1
Complete Investor KYC
Identity, risk profile, and compliance checks
2
Fund Wallet
Deposit capital and review allocation limits
3
Review Asset Detail
Confirm discount, tenor, buyer quality, and historical performance
4
Invest & Mint Tokens
Receive tokens at purchase price and settle at face value maturity
How It Works (By Stakeholder)
SMEUploads invoice and shipment evidence to request financing.
Verification NetworkValidates shipment, customs, invoice and insurance data.
InvestorEvaluates risk/return and purchases tokenized receivable units.
BuyerPays invoice at maturity; principal and return are distributed.
